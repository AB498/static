FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

HOW DO I MAKE A CLAIM?

To start the process of a claim, an initial conversation is required about what happened and the injuries you sustained as a result before discussing how Cordell and Co can help. Simply call us on 01306 879768 to speak to our new business team to discuss your potential case.

WHAT CAN I CLAIM FOR?

For a personal injury you can claim for pain and suffering, loss of earnings and future loss of earnings. We can also claim for expenses from your accident such as travel costs and damaged clothing. The severity of your injures and the extent of the affect it has had on your life are factors which are assessed when valuing your claim. This is always obtained from a medical report conducted by an expert and calculated by citation to previous case law.

HOW LONG DO I HAVE TO MAKE A CLAIM?

The most common rule is that a person has 3 years from the date of their accident to settle the claim or have issued court proceedings. In other cases, if the individual was under the age of 18 at the time of their accident, they have 3 years from their 18th birthday to have issued court proceedings or settled the claim. Additionally, there are some alterations for a time limit which we can assess on a case by case basis. The best thing to do is to give us a call on 01306 879768 and a member of our specialist team will be able to advise you of the time limit that applies to your accident.

DOES EVERY CASE GO TO COURT?

Most personal injury claims are settled outside of court, very few cases will be in front of a judge. Only 3% of our cases will go to court proceedings.
